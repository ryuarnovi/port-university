My Story

Introduction

Hello! My name is Rizki Ardiansyah Novianto, and I am a passionate computer science student at Universitas Dian Nuswantoro. I have always been fascinated by technology and how it can be used to solve real-world problems.

My Journey

My journey in the field of technology began at a young age when I first learned to code. I started with simple HTML and CSS, creating my own personal website. As I grew older, my interest expanded to include programming languages like Python and Java, as well as web development frameworks.

Throughout my academic career, I have participated in various coding competitions and hackathons, which have helped me hone my skills and collaborate with like-minded individuals. These experiences have not only enhanced my technical abilities but also taught me the importance of teamwork and perseverance.

Significant Experiences

One of the most significant milestones in my journey was when I secured an internship at a local tech startup. During my time there, I worked on developing a web application that streamlined internal processes, which was a rewarding experience that solidified my desire to pursue a career in software development.

Additionally, I have been actively involved in community service, teaching coding to high school students. This experience has been incredibly fulfilling, as it allows me to give back and inspire the next generation of tech enthusiasts.
